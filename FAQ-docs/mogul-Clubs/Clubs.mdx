## Welcome to mogul Clubs

mogul Clubs are integrated social communities within the mogul platform designed specifically for real-estate investors. Inside each club, members connect, share knowledge, discuss strategies, and collectively benefit from platform activity.

> **Quick take:** Join a community, invest together, earn rewards.

### Why join or start a club?

- **Community & Connection** – Engage with like-minded investors, build relationships, and share experiences.  
- **Enhanced Learning** – Leverage collective wisdom and diverse perspectives.  
- **Collective Rewards** – Earn a share of the 2 % club reward on member investments.  
- **Focused Discussions** – Create or find clubs around specific strategies or niches.  
- **Leadership Opportunities** – Become a **Chief Mogul** and steer your own community.
