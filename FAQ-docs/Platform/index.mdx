## Welcome to mogul

mogul is the easiest way to build a diversified real-estate portfolio. Invest fractionally, earn monthly rental income, capture appreciation, vote on key decisions, and enjoy real-estate tax advantages — all through a user-friendly dashboard and vibrant community.

> **Quick take:** Buy shares in curated properties, track performance in real time, and connect with fellow investors via mogul Clubs.
