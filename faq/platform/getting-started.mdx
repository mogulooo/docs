<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Account Creation" titleSize="h2">
    Visit **mogul.club** and sign up with your email + secure password — it takes ~30 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Profile Completion" titleSize="h2">
    Answer a few quick questions about your investing goals and (optionally) book a welcome call.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Identity Verification (KYC)" titleSize="h2">
    As a regulated platform we must verify identity. Provide basic info + SSN (or equivalent ID if non-US). Our trusted vendor handles data securely, and the check finishes in under a minute.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Explore Properties" titleSize="h2">
    You’re in! Browse live offerings and read the deep-dive pages to make an informed choice.
  </Step>
</Steps>
