---
title: "Investing Step-by-Step"
description: "From selecting a property to funding your stake."
---

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step 1: Browse Listings" titleSize="h2">
    Click any property to see location, strategy, projections, and legal docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Review the Details" titleSize="h2">
    Investment rationale, market overview, IRR, cash-on-cash yield, inspection reports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 3: Click “Invest”" titleSize="h2">
    Start the commitment flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 4: Choose Amount" titleSize="h2">
    From \$250 to \$250k, we have seen it all; determine the amount that makes sense for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 5: Complete Profile & KYC" titleSize="h2">
    One-time if you haven’t already.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 6: Link Payment Method" titleSize="h2">
    ACH, wire, debit card, credit card, PayPal, Venmo, or **USDT (Tether on the Avalanche Blockchain)**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 7: Confirm Investment" titleSize="h2">
    Review and accept.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 8: Fund & Close" titleSize="h2">
    Money moves, ownership is recorded, and you’re done.
  </Step>
</Steps>
