---
title: "After You Invest"
description: "What happens once your funds clear and the property goes live."
---

### Ownership Confirmation

You receive digital confirmation of membership interest in the **property-specific LLC**.

### Cash-Flow Timeline

When the property is operational, your share of net rental income hits your mogul wallet **on the second Tuesday of the following month**.

### Monthly Valuations

Dashboard updates every month with fresh third-party appraisal-level data.

### Tax Documents

Schedule K-1s and other forms are delivered in-app at year-end.

### Governance

For significant decisions (typically \>\$1,000) you can vote pro-rata to your stake.

### Ongoing Updates

Expect email \+ dashboard memos on performance, maintenance, or market news.
